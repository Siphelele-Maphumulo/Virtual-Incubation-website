Our Story

Witnessing firsthand the vast tech knowledge gap.

Born from a 2023 internship experience, Thembelihle I.T Solutions founder, Siphelele Audacious Maphumulo, witnessed firsthand the vast tech knowledge gap among South African youth. Witnessing students unfamiliar with terms like Artificial Intelligence and the 4th Industrial Revolution ignited a passion within Siphelele to bridge this gap. He envisioned a future where young people, equipped with cutting-edge tech skills, could not only access promising careers but also become innovators and creators, driving progress within their communities and beyond.

Our Strategies

Transforming Tech Education with Mobile Labs.

Beyond brick-and-mortar workshops, Thembelihle I.T Solutions envisions a future equipped with a fleet of state-of-the-art mobile IT labs. These 6m x 6m containers, outfitted with 12 high-performance computers, projectors, whiteboards, Wi-Fi, and photocopying facilities, will act as self-contained tech oases. Mounted on 4x4 trailers, these labs will traverse geographical barriers, bringing our transformative programs directly to rural communities often marginalized from tech exposure

This innovative approach offers several strategic advantages:

  • Accessibility: Bridging the physical divide, the mobile labs will reach previously inaccessible areas, democratizing tech education and ensuring rural youth aren't left behind in the digital revolution.
  • Agility: Rapidly deployable and adaptable, the labs can cater to fluctuating student populations and diverse community needs.
  • Immersive Learning: The container structure fosters a focused and collaborative learning environment, free from distractions, enhancing student engagement and knowledge retention.
  • Technology Integration: Wi-Fi access empowers participation in webinars, summits, and live tech talks, keeping students up-to-date with the latest advancements.
  • Resource Sharing: Photocopying facilities provide valuable document access, promoting effective learning and project documentation.

By investing in this mobile lab initiative, you're not just supporting a company, you're investing in a sustainable, scalable, and impactful model for tech education. You're empowering rural youth with the tools and knowledge to become active participants in the digital economy, propelling South Africa towards a more inclusive and innovative future

Remember, these mobile labs go beyond classrooms. They represent beacon's of opportunity, igniting imaginations and fostering a generation of tech-savvy changemakers in even the most remote corners of the country.
